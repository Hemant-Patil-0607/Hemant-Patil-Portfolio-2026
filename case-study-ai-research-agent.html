An AI-first workspace designed to help startup founders move from scattered information to one intelligent environment for research, strategy, planning, and decision-making.

01 — The Problem

Startup founders operate in chaos — juggling multiple browser tabs, disconnected tools, scattered notes, and endless research. There's no single environment that connects research, strategy, planning, and execution.

02 — The Solution

Build an AI-native workspace that centralizes research, synthesizes information, supports strategic thinking, and connects directly to execution — all in one intelligent environment.

Strategy & Planning Systems

From research insights to strategic decisions and actionable plans — a connected workflow for founders.

Decision Tracking

Track decisions, their context, and outcomes — building institutional knowledge over time.
